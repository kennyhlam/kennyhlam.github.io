About

I graduated from MIT in 2014 with my M.Eng in Computer Science & Engineering; before that, I finished my bachelor's in 2013 with Physics and Computer Science. I have a variety of technical interests, including distributed systems/algorithms, database systems, machine learning, and computational photography.

In my free time, I like to blog—about both technical things and random thoughts that cross my mind. There's also a few other hobbies that I picked up, including photography, basketball, frisbee, and rock climbing (yikes, this is quickly turning into a dating profile!). I also try to help out with important causes whenever I can.

The easiest way to reach me is always via email.
